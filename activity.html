Honest Opinion

In all seriousness, I love every aspect of Overwatch. This particular game is the very reason I'm pursuing a career in computer science. It's beautifully developed with so much intricacy.

Even the characters have unique and detailed backstories. Lore is not presented in-game, but can be put together from officially released animated shorts, comics, etc.

The game was released in 2015, but it still has a very active (and toxic) community. I have been playing for 3 years now (1,105 hours total), and it still manages to give me a rush of adrenaline everytime. Overwatch 2 is under development, so I have no intentions of straying away from the franchise anytime soon.
